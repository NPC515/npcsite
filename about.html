About Our Church

Learn more about our history, mission, and leadership

Our Vision

Committed to fulfilling the great commission by Evangelizing Kenya and the rest of the world.

  • Spreading the Gospel
  • Building Communities
  • Global Impact

Our Mission

Teaching and disciplining people in the true doctrine of the word of God.

  • Biblical Teaching
  • Discipleship
  • Spiritual Growth

Our History

The journey of Noble Pentecostal Church through the years

1993

Our Humble Beginnings

Noble Pentecostal Church began as a small prayer group of 10 families meeting in the home of Pastor Jonathan Kimutai.

1998

First Church Building

As the congregation grew, we acquired our first permanent location in Eldoret town, establishing our presence in the community.

2005

Community Outreach Begins

We launched our first major outreach programs, including food distribution, medical camps, and educational support for underprivileged children.

2012

Expansion to Multiple Branches

The church expanded with new branches in Kitagwa, Chepsonoi, and Kapkangani, extending our ministry to reach more communities.

2019

New Main Sanctuary

We dedicated our new main sanctuary in Eldoret, a modern facility to accommodate our growing congregation and expand our ministries.

Present Day

Continuing Our Mission

Today, Noble Pentecostal Church continues to grow spiritually and numerically, focusing on discipleship, community service, and spreading the gospel.
