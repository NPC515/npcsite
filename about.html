Our History

The journey of Noble Pentecostal Church through the years

1999

Our Humble Beginnings

We began as a fellowship at a shop in chepsonoi.

2000

Plot Acquisition & Registration

Moved to a temporary church after acquiring a plot and registered the fellowship into Nandi Pentecostal Church.

2006

Sanctuary Construction

Construction of the main sanctuary that took 3 years.

2009

Sanctuary Dedication

The main sanctuary was dedicated on 29th August 2009 by Bishop Boniface Adoyo of Citam. The occasion was graced by the then Deputy Prime Minister, Hon. Musalia Mudavadi.

2016

Kapkangani Branch Establishment

Opened the second branch at kapkangani.

2018

Kitagwa Branch Establishment

Opened the third branch in Kitagwa

?

Njathaini Branch Establishment

Opened the fourth branch in Njathaini

2024

Marurui Branch Establishment

Opened the fifth branch in Marurui

Present Day

Continuing Our Mission

Today, Noble Pentecostal Church continues to grow spiritually and numerically, focusing on discipleship, community service, and spreading the gospel.
