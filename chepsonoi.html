Chepsonoi Branch

Our main branch serving the community since 2005

Branch Overview

Established 2005

The Chepsonoi branch is the main headquarters of Noble Pentecostal Church, serving as our founding location and spiritual center.

Growing Community

Our doors are open to all who seek to know God and grow in their faith, fostering a welcoming environment for spiritual growth.

Ministry Operations

As our headquarters, this branch has been the center of our ministry operations and the spiritual home for hundreds of believers.
