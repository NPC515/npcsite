Our Giving Philosophy

At Noble Pentecostal Church, we believe that giving is an act of worship and a spiritual discipline that demonstrates our trust in God's provision. When we give generously, we participate in God's work and become channels of His blessing to others.

Church Operations

Supporting our facilities, utilities, and day-to-day activities that enable our worship services and community gatherings.

Community Outreach

Funding programs that serve the needy in our local community, including food drives and emergency assistance.

Educational Support

Helping underprivileged children access quality education through scholarships and school supplies.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

2 Corinthians 9:7
